WHAT DO WE DO?

24/7 continuous DNS server operation service is critical when doing business on the Internet.

•In the event of failure of your primary DNS server our secondary DNS service will provide backup and redundancy.

•Our secondary DNS service will provide additional backup DNS servers that mirror your own DNS zone information.This will improve your DNS resilience and enhance performance of your server web services in minutes to handle increased loads!

•If your primary or your other DNS servers go off-line because of maintenance or some disaster, our DNS servers will continue serving requests for your domain names..

•In the event of failure of your primary DNS server our secondary DNS service will provide backup and redundancy.

•Having secondary DNS servers off-site provides even better protection, since 2 servers on the same power-outlet or network connection could easily be affected by the same disaster (power failure, network outage, flooding, etc.).

•Our backup DNS servers providing Secondary DNS are located on physically and topologically separated networks to prevent any downtime caused by a single catastrophic event. With our Secondary DNS service you no longer have to wonder, "Is my DNS name server still up?" - even if yours goes down, we will continue to resolve your queries.

•If you are connecting to the Internet with a cable or DSL modem, you could easily run your own primary DNS server. However, most domain registrars require at least two DNS servers for each domain name.

By using this service, you will still have full control over your domain name's on your primary DNS server. Any changes or updates you make (on your primary server) are automatically and instantly transferred to our secondary servers (through standard zone transfers).
